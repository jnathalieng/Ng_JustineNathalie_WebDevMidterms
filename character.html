Character Introduction

Say hello to the Squeeziest Squad around!
Each bottle-top buddy has its own fruity flair and bubbly personality.
Collect them all and sip in style — there’s even a surprise flavor waiting to be discovered!

Berry Bop

Strawberry + Banana Flavor

Bouncy, bubbly, and always ready to jam — Berry Bop brings the party wherever they go! With a swirl of strawberry sass and banana giggles, this sweetie’s favorite move is the smoothie spin.

WIGGLE BOP

Wiggle Bop features the smooth, fruity flavor of Concord grapes. Known for their deep purple color and naturally sweet profile, this juice is a familiar go-to that’s bold, fun, and refreshingly simple.

SUNNY TWIST

Sunny Twist delivers the bright, tangy flavor of real orange juice with no added sugar. It’s made from sun-ripened citrus that brings a smooth, light tart taste — perfect for sunny afternoons.

MYSTI SWIRL

A magical mix of tropical fruits, Mysti Swirl keeps your taste buds guessing with every sip. Light, dreamy, and exciting — she’s the mystery in every bottle.

MANGO MAMBO

With a rich tropical blend of mango and pineapple, Mango Mambo is the life of the juice party. It’s smooth, sweet, and dances right into your heart with each sip.

APPLE WOBBLE

Apple Wobble brings crisp, orchard-fresh apple flavor with just a wobble of sweetness. Refreshing and juicy, it's the perfect pick for anytime energy.
